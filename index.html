About Me

I’m Claudia, a passionate creative, entrepreneur, and storyteller. I was born in Peru but raised in the U.S., and my roots play a big role in shaping my artistic vision. My love for art, fashion, and visual storytelling has guided me through many creative ventures—from running my own photography business to designing and creating content that inspires. Exploring web development introduced me to the technical side of design, and while I enjoyed working with elements like color theory and CSS, my true passion lies in fashion and digital content creation. My dream is to launch my own online boutique, where I can bring my original clothing designs to life blending elegance, confidence, and authenticity. Because of my background and heritage, I’m deeply inspired to weave culture, creativity, and storytelling into my work. Whether through styling, photography, painting, or producing videos, I love finding ways to merge my artistic influences into meaningful projects. This portfolio is a reflection of my journey past, present, and future as I continue to grow as a designer, businesswoman, and creator.

My Services

Photographer

I've always been a creative human since I was a child, long before I stumbled upon photography. I would draw/sketch and paint a lot until, Photography came into my life at age fifteen when my parents gifted me an affordable digital camera, and discovered another medium to express myself. Forward Fifteen years later I am the go-to photographer for Family and Friends. My Goal is to show the authentic beauty within in every shot. I want to capture images that evoke a sense of divine beauty, significance and worth. Documenting emotions is one of the most important parts of my work.
